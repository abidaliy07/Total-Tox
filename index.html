Drug Testing Methods & Panels

We offer multiple testing methods and customizable panels to meet your specific needs.

Urine Drug Testing

The most common and widely accepted method for drug testing. Available in multiple panel configurations.

Detection Window: 1-30 days depending on substance
Panels Available: 5, 7, 10, 12-panel
DOT Compliant: Yes, SAMHSA-certified

Hair Follicle Testing

Provides the longest detection window, ideal for pre-employment screening and long-term monitoring.

Detection Window: Up to 90 days
Panels Available: 5, 7, 10, 12-panel
Best For: Pre-employment, long-term monitoring

Oral Fluid/Saliva Testing

Non-invasive method that's difficult to adulterate. Fast results and convenient collection.

Detection Window: 24-48 hours
Panels Available: 5, 7, 10-panel
DOT Compliant: Yes, DOT-approved method

Blood Drug Testing

Most accurate method for recent drug use detection. Used for legal and medical purposes.

Detection Window: Hours to days
Accuracy: Highest accuracy available
Best For: Legal cases, medical purposes

Drug Test Panels Explained

5-Panel Drug Test

Standard DOT-required test screens for:

  • Marijuana (THC)
  • Cocaine
  • Amphetamines
  • Opioids
  • PCP

7-Panel Drug Test

Includes 5-panel plus:

  • Benzodiazepines
  • Barbiturates

10-Panel Drug Test

Comprehensive screening includes:

  • All 7-panel substances
  • Methadone
  • Propoxyphene
  • Methaqualone

12-Panel Drug Test

Most comprehensive includes:

  • All 10-panel substances
  • Ecstasy (MDMA)
  • Oxycodone
