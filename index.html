Drug Test Panels Explained

5-Panel Drug Test

Standard DOT-required test screens for:

  • Marijuana (THC)
  • Cocaine
  • Amphetamines
  • Opioids
  • PCP
Most common for DOT and pre-employment

7-Panel Drug Test

Includes 5-panel plus:

  • Benzodiazepines
  • Barbiturates
Expanded screening for comprehensive testing

10-Panel Drug Test

Comprehensive screening includes:

  • All 7-panel substances
  • Methadone
  • Propoxyphene
  • Methaqualone
Most comprehensive standard panel

12-Panel Drug Test

Most comprehensive includes:

  • All 10-panel substances
  • Ecstasy (MDMA)
  • Oxycodone
Maximum coverage for workplace safety
