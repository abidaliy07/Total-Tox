Urine Drug Testing

Urine drug testing remains the gold standard for workplace drug screening. Our urine testing services include:

  • 5-Panel Testing: Tests for marijuana, cocaine, PCP, amphetamines, and opiates
  • 10-Panel Testing: Includes additional substances like benzodiazepines, barbiturates, and methadone
  • 13-Panel Testing: Most comprehensive panel including synthetic drugs and prescription medications
  • Instant Testing: On-site results in minutes for rapid screening
  • Lab-Based Testing: GC/MS confirmation for definitive results

DOT Drug Testing

Full compliance with FMCSA regulations for commercial motor vehicle operators:

  • Pre-Employment: Required before drivers can operate commercial vehicles
  • Random Testing: Unannounced testing throughout the year (50% of drivers annually)
  • Post-Accident: Required after qualifying accidents
  • Reasonable Suspicion: When trained supervisors observe signs of drug/alcohol use
  • Return-to-Duty: After completing substance abuse program
  • Follow-Up: Ongoing testing after return to work

Non-DOT Drug Testing
